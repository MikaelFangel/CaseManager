    risk_badge(assigns)

    Renders a badge showing risk or severity level.

    Severity come in five level – critical, high, medium, low and info. This attribute is required.

    Example

    <.risk_badge colour={:critical} />
<.risk_badge colour={:high} />
<.risk_badge colour={:medium} />
<.risk_badge colour={:low} />
<.risk_badge colour={:info} />

    Attributes

    • colour (:atom) (required) - Must be one of :critical, :high, :medium, :low, or :info.
    • class (:string) - Defaults to nil.
    • Global attributes are accepted.
